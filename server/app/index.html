The Research Society Manipal

Our goal is to foster a thriving research community that drives interdisciplinary innovation while bridging the gap between students and alumni worldwide. By leveraging the extensive research expertise of our alumni, we aim to create meaningful collaborations and unlock opportunities for groundbreaking advancements.

Our Vision

To cultivate a thriving research ecosystem that empowers students to push the boundaries of knowledge across diverse disciplines, fostering innovation and meaningful contributions to society.

Our Mission

To bridge students with global research opportunities, alumni expertise, and interdisciplinary collaboration while nurturing a culture of curiosity, critical thinking, and academic excellence.

Our Goal

To inspire and support students in publishing impactful research, securing internships at premier institutes, and driving innovation through accessible events, guidance, and resources.

Our Domains

Exploring diverse domains to foster innovation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and impactful research.

AI & Cybersecurity

AI, an interdisciplinary science, has revolutionized life through advancements in machine learning and deep learning. At RSM, we explore subfields like NLP, CV, Reinforcement Learning, GANs, Federated Learning, and cybersecurity.

Electronics

Electronics combines physics, nanomaterials, quantum mechanics, signal processing, circuit design, and algorithms. We explore the nano realm to enable advancements like nanoscale devices, quantum computing, and neuromorphic computing.

Biotechnology

Biotechnology is the sweet spot between Biology, Technology and Engineering. It's the area of study responsible for production of medicines, environmental control, vaccines, cancer research and many more fields

REHT & Chemical

Chemical engineering opens paths to fields like Nanotechnology, Drug Delivery, Thermodynamics, and Renewable Energy. We aim to tackle 21st-century challenges such as climate change, vaccine development, and water scarcity.

Maths & Physics

Mathematics and physics form the foundation for understanding the universe, from quantum mechanics to complex algorithms. Our research delves into areas like theoretical physics, applied mathematics, and advanced modeling to solve real-world problems and drive innovation.

Aeronautical

Aeronautical engineering combines fluid dynamics, propulsion, materials science, and control systems to advance flight technology. Our research focuses on areas like aircraft design, aerospace propulsion, and aerodynamics to push the boundaries of air and space travel.

Robotics

Robotics is an integration of maths, physics, and engineering fields. At RSM, we are focused on intense research in the fields of perception, planning, kinematics sprawling across autonomous systems, multi-agent systems, biomedical robots to name a few.

Web Development and Graphics

Web Development and Graphics encompass creating dynamic websites, designing visually compelling content, and managing digital presence and showcasing the club's activities and engage the community.
